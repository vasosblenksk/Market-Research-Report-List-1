<p><p>The carbon dioxide (CO2) monitor market includes various types, notably End-Tidal Carbon Dioxide (EtCO2) monitors and Transcutaneous Carbon Dioxide (tcpCO2) monitors. EtCO2 monitors measure the concentration of carbon dioxide in exhaled breath, primarily used in anesthesia and critical care settings for real-time monitoring of respiratory function. TcpCO2 monitors assess carbon dioxide levels through the skin, providing continuous non-invasive monitoring, often used in neonatal care and patients with respiratory diseases, ensuring efficient management of carbon dioxide levels in different clinical scenarios.</p></p>
