<p><p>The automotive power steering pump market comprises three primary types: vane, roller, and slipper pumps. Vane pumps utilize sliding vanes within a rotor to create hydraulic pressure, providing smooth steering assistance. Roller pumps employ cylindrical rollers to enhance efficiency and reduce noise, making them suitable for modern vehicles. Slipper pumps utilize sliding elements to optimize performance under varying loads, offering improved responsiveness. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle maneuverability and driver comfort, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
