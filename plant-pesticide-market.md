<p><p>The plant pesticide market comprises various types, including phytotoxins, phytogenous insect hormones, and others. Phytotoxins are natural or synthetic compounds derived from plants that disrupt pest metabolism or growth, effectively minimizing their impact on crops. Phytogenous insect hormones mimic natural hormones in insects, disrupting their development and reproduction, thus controlling pest populations. Other market types include beneficial microorganisms and biological agents that enhance plant resilience or target specific pests without harming the environment, fostering sustainable agriculture.</p></p>
