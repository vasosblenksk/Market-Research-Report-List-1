<p><p>The kids orthodontics market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Dental Braces** are traditional metal or ceramic devices that align teeth through brackets and wires.</p><p>2. **Aligners** are clear, removable trays that gradually shift teeth without the visibility of braces.</p><p>3. **Retainers** are used post-treatment to maintain teeth positions and prevent shifting. </p><p>4. **Dental Wires** connect brackets in braces, essential for applying pressure to teeth during alignment. Each component plays a vital role in achieving optimal dental alignment in children.</p></p>
