<p><p>The Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene (BOPE) film market comprises two primary types: monolayer and composite films. Monolayer films are made from a single layer of BOPE, providing excellent clarity, strength, and moisture resistance, ideal for packaging applications. Composite films, on the other hand, combine multiple layers of different materials, enhancing barrier properties and flexibility, making them suitable for more specialized applications. Both types leverage the unique properties of BOPE, offering improved performance and sustainability in packaging solutions.</p></p>
