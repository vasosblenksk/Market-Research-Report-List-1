<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Proportional Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Valves with Bender Element</li><li>Jet Valves with Multilayer Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric proportional valve market consists of two main types: valves with bender elements and jet valves with multilayer actuators. Valves with bender elements utilize piezoelectric materials to convert electrical signals into mechanical movement, allowing precise flow control. In contrast, jet valves with multilayer actuators employ multiple layers of piezoelectric materials to achieve rapid actuation and high responsiveness. These technologies enable efficient fluid control in various applications, including automotive, industrial automation, and aerospace, enhancing performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
