<p><strong>The Animal-specific High-frequency Electrosurgery Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal-specific high-frequency electrosurgery device market comprises unipolar and bipolar types. Unipolar devices utilize a single electrode to perform surgery, directing current through the animal's body to the grounding pad, making them suitable for larger surgical areas. In contrast, bipolar devices employ two electrodes, allowing current to pass only between them, which minimizes tissue damage and is ideal for precise, localized procedures. Both types cater to different surgical needs in veterinary medicine, enhancing precision and safety.</p></p>
