<p><p>An Immersed Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) is an advanced wastewater treatment technology that combines biological treatment and membrane filtration. <p><p>The Immersed Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) market is segmented into three primary types based on membrane design: hollow fiber membranes, flat sheet membranes, and multi-tubular membranes. Hollow fiber membranes, known for their high surface area and compactness, are widely used in wastewater treatment. Flat sheet membranes offer easier cleaning and maintenance, making them suitable for various applications. Multi-tubular membranes, with multiple passages, provide enhanced flow rates and durability, catering to specific industrial needs. Each type serves distinct operational requirements and efficiencies.</p></p>
