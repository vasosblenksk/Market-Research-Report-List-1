<p><p>The primary nickel market consists of two main types: natural primary nickel and recycled primary nickel. Natural primary nickel is extracted from mining operations, where ores like laterite and sulfide are processed to produce nickel metal. In contrast, recycled primary nickel comes from the recovery and refining of nickel-containing scrap materials, such as spent batteries or industrial waste. Both markets play crucial roles in supplying nickel for various applications, including stainless steel production and battery manufacturing, aiding resource sustainability.</p></p>
