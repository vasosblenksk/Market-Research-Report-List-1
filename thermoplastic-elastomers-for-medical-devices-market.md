<p><p>Thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) used in medical devices are categorized into several types. Styrene-based TPEs (SBCs) offer excellent flexibility and aesthetics, making them suitable for various applications. Thermoplastic polyolefins feature good chemical resistance and are lightweight, ideal for medical tubing. Thermoplastic polyurethanes provide superior elasticity and abrasion resistance, often used in skin-contact devices. Polyether ester TPEs (TPEE) combine soft-touch characteristics with durability. Other TPEs include specialty blends tailored for specific medical needs, enhancing device performance and patient comfort.</p></p>
