<p><p>The plasma etching agents market is categorized into two main types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents involve liquid chemicals that remove material from surfaces, typically used for bulk material etching in various applications. In contrast, dry etching agents utilize plasma technology to etch materials through gas-phase reactions, offering higher precision and control, which is crucial for semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication processes. Both types play essential roles in shaping and patterning materials in advanced manufacturing.</p></p>
