<p><strong>The Post Processor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>G-code Post-processor</li><li>CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing) Post-processor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Post Processor market primarily includes two types: G-code Post-processors and CAM Post-processors. G-code Post-processors translate CAD designs into G-code, a language that CNC machines understand, ensuring precise execution of complex tasks. CAM Post-processors, on the other hand, integrate with CAM software, facilitating the transition from digital design to physical production by generating machine-specific code that optimizes manufacturing processes. Together, these tools enhance efficiency, accuracy, and automation in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
