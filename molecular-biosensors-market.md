<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Biosensors</li><li>Optical Biosensors</li><li>Thermal Biosensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular biosensors market comprises various types, each utilizing different mechanisms for detecting biological molecules. Electrochemical biosensors measure the electrical signals produced by biochemical reactions, making them ideal for monitoring glucose levels. Optical biosensors use light to detect changes in refractive index or fluorescence, suitable for numerous applications, including drug testing. Thermal biosensors assess temperature changes related to biochemical interactions. Piezoelectric biosensors exploit mass changes on a sensor's surface, offering sensitive detection for pathogens and biomolecules.</p></p>
