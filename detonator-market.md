<p><p>The detonator market can be categorized into three primary types: Industrial Electric Detonators, Shock Tube Detonators, and Others. Industrial Electric Detonators utilize electrical impulses for precise initiation, making them ideal for large-scale construction and mining operations. Shock Tube Detonators use a thin plastic tube containing a reactive powder that transmits a shock wave, offering a safer alternative with no risk of accidental detonation from electricity. The “Others” segment encompasses specialized or emerging detonator technologies, catering to niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
