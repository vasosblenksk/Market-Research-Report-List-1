<p><strong>The Door Closer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Applied Door Closer</li><li>Concealed Door Closer</li><li>Floor Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The door closer market comprises three main types: surface applied door closers, concealed door closers, and floor springs. Surface applied door closers are mounted on the door's surface, offering visibility and ease of installation. Concealed door closers are embedded within the door or frame, providing a sleek, unobtrusive appearance. Floor springs, installed in the floor, facilitate door closure with minimal visibility while accommodating heavier doors. Each type caters to different aesthetic preferences and functional requirements in various settings.</p></p>
