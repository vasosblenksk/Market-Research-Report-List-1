<p><p>The PCB terminal block market comprises various types, notably "Header & Plug" and "Receptacle" configurations. Header & Plug setups feature a male header soldered onto the PCB, paired with a female plug, facilitating easy connections and disconnections. Receptacle types are designed for direct attachment to the PCB, allowing for secure connections with external wiring. Both configurations are essential for ensuring reliable electrical connections in various applications, from consumer electronics to industrial equipment, enhancing functionality and maintenance efficiency.</p></p>
