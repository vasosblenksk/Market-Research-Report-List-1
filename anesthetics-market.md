<p><ul><li>Local Anesthetics</li><li>Regional Anesthetics</li><li>General Anesthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anesthetics market is categorized into three main types: local, regional, and general anesthetics. Local anesthetics numb a specific area of the body for minor procedures without affecting overall consciousness. Regional anesthetics block sensation in larger body regions, often used in surgeries of the limbs or abdomen. General anesthetics induce a state of unconsciousness, affecting the whole body, making them essential for major surgeries. Each type has distinct applications tailored to patient needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>
