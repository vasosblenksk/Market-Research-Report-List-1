The ultra-short baseline hydroacoustic positioning system accurately tracks underwater equipment using sound waves. The acoustic Doppler velocity meter (ADCP) measures water current velocity and flow direction. Additionally, other market instruments include single-beam systems, underwater cameras, and remotely operated vehicles, enhancing marine research and navigation capabilities.</p></p>
