<p><strong>The Lighting Contactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Held</li><li>Mechanically Held</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting contactors market is categorized into two main types: electrically held and mechanically held contactors. Electrically held contactors utilize an electrical signal to maintain their closed state, providing convenience and ease of control, but may consume power continuously. In contrast, mechanically held contactors rely on mechanical means to remain in position, resulting in reduced energy consumption and enhanced reliability during power interruptions. Both types serve essential roles in controlling lighting systems efficiently while catering to varying operational needs.</p></p>
