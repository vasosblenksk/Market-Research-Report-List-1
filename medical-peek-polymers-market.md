<p><p>The Medical PEEK (polyether ether ketone) polymers market is primarily segmented into two types: powder and particle forms. PEEK powder is utilized in additive manufacturing and custom medical devices, providing versatility and precision in production. Conversely, PEEK particles, often in the form of granules or pellets, are used for traditional processing techniques such as injection molding and extrusion. Both forms offer excellent biocompatibility, mechanical strength, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for various medical applications, including implants and surgical instruments.</p></p>
